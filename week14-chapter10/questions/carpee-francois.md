## Question

intentions when poor decisions can be explained by stupidity. But doesn't 
this assume your boss acts in good faith, just poorly?

listening and start protecting yourself? How can you tell the difference 
between an incompetent boss and a malicious one?

want a functional relationship. If your boss benefits from dysfunction,
